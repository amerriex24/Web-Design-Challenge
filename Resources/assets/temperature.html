Latitude vs Max Temperature

Latitude has a relatively strong effect on temperature based off the data. There are of course outliers that indicate that there are other factors that have an effect on the temperatures other than the latitude, but in general cities closer to the equator are warmer than those cities that are further to the north and south.

This graph might be a bit skewed, because this data was taken during winter time, so of course nothern cities will have lower temps.
